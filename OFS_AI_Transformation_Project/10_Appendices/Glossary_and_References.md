## Glossary of Terms

### A

**ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act)**
Federal law requiring accessibility compliance in commercial spaces, including minimum clearances, accessible routes, and ergonomic considerations for furniture placement.

**Agentic System**
An AI system composed of multiple specialized agents that work together autonomously to accomplish complex tasks. Each agent has specific capabilities and can coordinate with other agents to solve problems.

**API (Application Programming Interface)**
A set of protocols and tools that allow different software applications to communicate with each other. OFS uses REST APIs and GraphQL for integrations.

**AR (Augmented Reality)**
Technology that overlays digital information onto the real world through devices like smartphones or AR glasses. Used in OFS platform for visualizing furniture in actual spaces.

### B

**BIM (Building Information Modeling)**
A digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of a building. Common formats include Revit (.rvt), IFC (.ifc), and DWG (.dwg).

**BIFMA (Business and Institutional Furniture Manufacturers Association)**
Organization that develops standards for furniture quality, safety, and sustainability. BIFMA LEVEL certification measures environmental and social impacts.

**Budget Scenario**
Different configuration options for a project at varying price points (e.g., Conservative, Balanced, Premium), each optimized for different priorities.

### C

**Carbon Footprint**
Total greenhouse gas emissions caused by an individual, organization, event, or product, expressed as carbon dioxide equivalent (COâ‚‚e).

**Certifications**
Third-party verifications that products meet specific standards:
- **GREENGUARD Gold:** Low chemical emissions
- **FSC Certified:** Sustainable wood sourcing
- **BIFMA LEVEL 3:** Comprehensive sustainability
- **Cradle to Cradle:** Circular design principles

**ClickHouse**
Open-source column-oriented database management system designed for online analytical processing (OLAP). Used in OFS platform for analytics and reporting.

**Confidence Score**
A numerical value (0.00 to 1.00) indicating how certain the AI is about a recommendation or decision. Scores above 0.85 are considered "high confidence."

**Conversational AI**
AI technology that enables natural language interactions between humans and computers, powered by large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4 or Claude.

### D

**Dealer**
An authorized OFS reseller who works with clients to specify, sell, and install OFS products. Dealers are critical partners in the sales ecosystem.

**Deep Learning**
A subset of machine learning using neural networks with multiple layers to learn complex patterns from data. Used in computer vision and recommendation systems.

**Detectron2**
Facebook AI Research's computer vision framework for object detection and segmentation. Used in OFS platform to analyze floor plans.

### E

**Embedding**
A mathematical representation of text, images, or other data as vectors in high-dimensional space. Similar items have similar embeddings, enabling semantic search and recommendations.

**End-of-Life (EOL)**
The final stage of a product's lifecycle when it's disposed of or recycled. Sustainable furniture considers EOL recyclability.

**Ergonomics**
The study of designing furniture and workspaces to fit human needs, reducing strain and improving comfort and productivity.

### F

**Facilities Manager**
A professional responsible for managing an organization's physical workspace, including furniture procurement, space planning, and workplace operations.

**Floor Plan**
A scaled diagram showing the layout of a space from above, including walls, doors, windows, and dimensions. Input format for OFS AI platform.

**FSC (Forest Stewardship Council)**
International organization that sets standards for responsible forest management. FSC-certified furniture uses wood from sustainably managed forests.

### G

**GraphQL**
A query language for APIs that allows clients to request exactly the data they need. Alternative to REST APIs, used in OFS platform for flexible data retrieval.

**GREENGUARD Gold**
Certification for products with low chemical emissions, ensuring better indoor air quality. Important for healthcare and education environments.

### H

**Heatmap**
A data visualization technique using color to represent values, such as natural light levels, traffic patterns, or space utilization in floor plans.

**HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning)**
Building systems that control temperature and air quality. Furniture placement must consider HVAC zones and airflow.

### I

**IFC (Industry Foundation Classes)**
An open file format for BIM data, enabling interoperability between different CAD and design software applications.

**Inference**
The process of using a trained machine learning model to make predictions or decisions on new data. Real-time inference powers AI recommendations.

**IRR (Internal Rate of Return)**
A financial metric used to estimate the profitability of an investment. Higher IRR indicates better return. OFS platform achieves 312% IRR.

### J

**JSON (JavaScript Object Notation)**
A lightweight data format for exchanging information between systems. Used extensively in OFS APIs.

**JWT (JSON Web Token)**
A compact, URL-safe means of representing claims to be transferred between two parties. Used for secure authentication in OFS platform.

### K

**KPI (Key Performance Indicator)**
A measurable value that demonstrates how effectively an organization is achieving business objectives. Examples: win rate, sales cycle time, customer satisfaction.

**Kubernetes (K8s)**
An open-source container orchestration platform for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

### L

**LangChain / LangGraph**
Frameworks for building applications with large language models, enabling multi-agent workflows, memory, and tool usage.

**LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design)**
Green building certification program recognizing sustainable building practices. Furniture contributes to LEED credits.

**LLM (Large Language Model)**
AI models trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human language. Examples: GPT-4, Claude 3.5 Sonnet.

### M

**ML (Machine Learning)**
A subset of AI where systems learn from data without explicit programming. Used for product recommendations, space optimization, and predictive analytics.

**Microservices**
An architectural style where applications are composed of small, independent services that communicate via APIs. OFS platform uses microservices architecture.

**MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)**
The smallest number of units a manufacturer or dealer will sell at one time. Affects pricing and availability.

### N

**Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
AI technology for understanding, interpreting, and generating human language. Powers conversational AI in OFS platform.

**NPV (Net Present Value)**
Financial metric that calculates the present value of future cash flows. OFS platform achieves $264M NPV over 5 years.

**Neural Network**
A machine learning model inspired by human brain structure, consisting of interconnected nodes (neurons) organized in layers.

### O

**OAuth 2.0**
An authorization framework enabling secure, delegated access to user resources without sharing passwords. Used for third-party integrations.

**OLAP (Online Analytical Processing)**
Database systems optimized for complex queries and analytics. ClickHouse provides OLAP capabilities for OFS platform.

**OLTP (Online Transaction Processing)**
Database systems optimized for transaction-oriented applications. PostgreSQL provides OLTP capabilities for OFS platform.

**Open Office**
A workspace layout without traditional cubicles or private offices, featuring shared workstations and collaboration areas.

### P

**Payback Period**
The time required to recover the cost of an investment. OFS platform achieves 10-month payback period.

**Persona**
A fictional character representing a user type, used in design to understand user needs, behaviors, and goals.

**PostgreSQL**
Open-source relational database management system known for reliability, feature robustness, and performance. Primary database for OFS platform.

**Progressive Disclosure**
A design pattern that shows only essential information initially, revealing additional details as needed. Reduces cognitive overload.

### Q

**Query Optimization**
The process of improving database query performance through better indexing, query structure, and execution plans.

### R

**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**
AI technique that combines information retrieval with text generation, enabling LLMs to access external knowledge bases.

**Redis**
Open-source, in-memory data structure store used as a database, cache, and message broker. Provides caching and session management for OFS platform.

**Recommendation Engine**
Machine learning system that suggests products or actions based on user preferences, historical data, and contextual factors.

**REST (Representational State Transfer)**
Architectural style for building web APIs using standard HTTP methods (GET, POST, PUT, DELETE).

**ROI (Return on Investment)**
Financial metric measuring the gain or loss from an investment relative to its cost. OFS platform delivers 1,144% Year 1 ROI.

### S

**SaaS (Software as a Service)**
Software licensing model where applications are hosted centrally and accessed via the internet. OFS platform is delivered as SaaS.

**SAML (Security Assertion Markup Language)**
Open standard for exchanging authentication and authorization data. Used for enterprise single sign-on (SSO).

**Semantic Search**
Search technique that understands the meaning and context of queries, not just keyword matching. Powered by embeddings.

**Spec Sheet**
A detailed document listing product specifications, dimensions, materials, finishes, and pricing.

**SSO (Single Sign-On)**
Authentication scheme allowing users to log in once and access multiple applications. Reduces password fatigue and improves security.

**Sustainability Score**
A numerical rating (0-100) reflecting a product's environmental impact, including recycled content, emissions, energy use, and recyclability.

### T

**TAM (Total Addressable Market)**
The total revenue opportunity available for a product or service. US commercial furniture market TAM is $15.2B.

**Terraform**
Infrastructure-as-code tool for building, changing, and versioning cloud infrastructure safely and efficiently.

**TorchServe**
PyTorch model serving framework for deploying machine learning models at scale.

**Two-Tower Neural Network**
Machine learning architecture with separate "towers" for users and items, used in recommendation systems to learn embeddings.

**TypeScript**
Strongly-typed programming language that builds on JavaScript, adding static type definitions. Used in OFS frontend.

### U

**UI/UX (User Interface / User Experience)**
UI refers to visual elements users interact with; UX encompasses the entire experience of using a product.

**Upsell**
Sales technique of encouraging customers to purchase premium or additional products, increasing deal value.

### V

**Vector Database**
Specialized database for storing and querying high-dimensional vectors (embeddings), enabling semantic search and similarity matching.

**VR (Virtual Reality)**
Immersive technology creating computer-generated 3D environments. Used for virtual walkthroughs of furnished spaces.

### W

**WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)**
International standards for making web content accessible to people with disabilities. OFS platform targets WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.

**Webhook**
Automated message sent from one application to another when a specific event occurs. Used for real-time integrations.

**WELL Building Standard**
Certification focusing on human health and wellness in buildings, covering air, water, nourishment, light, fitness, comfort, and mind.

**Win Rate**
Percentage of sales opportunities that result in closed deals. Higher win rate indicates more effective sales process.

**Workstation**
A designated work area for an individual, typically including a desk, seating, and storage.

### X-Z

**XAI (Explainable AI)**
AI systems that provide human-understandable explanations for their decisions and recommendations, building trust and transparency.

## Standards & Certifications

### Environmental Certifications

**BIFMA LEVEL Certification**
- **Level 1:** Basic environmental criteria
- **Level 2:** Intermediate sustainability performance
- **Level 3:** Leadership sustainability performance (OFS target)

**LEED Credits (Furniture Contribution)**
- **MR Credit 3:** Materials reuse (1-2 points)
- **MR Credit 4:** Recycled content (1-2 points)
- **MR Credit 5:** Regional materials (1-2 points)
- **EQ Credit 4.4:** Low-emitting materials (1 point)

**GREENGUARD Certification**
- **GREENGUARD:** Low chemical emissions
- **GREENGUARD Gold:** Stricter emissions standards for sensitive environments (schools, healthcare)

**FSC Certification**
- **FSC 100%:** All wood from FSC-certified forests
- **FSC Mix:** Mix of certified and controlled sources
- **FSC Recycled:** All wood from recycled materials

### Quality & Safety Standards

**ANSI/BIFMA Standards**
- **ANSI/BIFMA X5.1:** Office Seating
- **ANSI/BIFMA X5.5:** Desk Products
- **ANSI/BIFMA X5.6:** Panel Systems
- **ANSI/BIFMA X5.9:** Storage Units
- **ANSI/BIFMA X5.11:** Large Occupant Office Chairs (>300 lbs capacity)

**Acoustics**
- **NRC (Noise Reduction Coefficient):** 0.0 (reflective) to 1.0 (absorptive)
- **ASTM C423:** Standard test method for sound absorption

### Accessibility Standards

**ADA Compliance**
- **Clear Width:** 36" minimum for accessible routes
- **Reach Ranges:** 15"-48" forward reach, 9"-54" side reach
- **Knee Clearance:** 27" minimum height, 30" minimum width
- **Work Surface Height:** 28"-34" adjustable range

**ANSI A117.1**
Accessible and Usable Buildings and Facilities
